Knowing About Us

  • Our Background

    Suhaz Sports, established in Alor Gajah, Melaka in 2014, is Malaysia’s largest company and one of Malaysia's major producers of sports footwear, clothing, and accessories. Suhaz Sports was owned by The Suhaz Group. The company also markets its products throughout the majority of Asian countries, including Indonesia, Brunei, and Singapore. Suhaz Sports expects that, with the ability to lead the company to three developed countries, the company will enter the worldwide market in the future. There are also more than 50 employees at the company. Suhaz Sports has earned more than RM 5 million annually in the sportswear business after eight years of operation. Suhaz Sports successfully increased brand exposure in 2018 through a collaboration with Christy Ng Shoes. The company also received the "Brand of the Year" Award for Malaysia from the World Branding Council, UK at a glittering ceremony in November 2019.
